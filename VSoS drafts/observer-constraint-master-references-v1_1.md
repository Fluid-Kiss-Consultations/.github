## Corrections log — changes from prior documents

Consolidated list of all corrections now canonical. When the outline, foundation memo, or application memo conflicts with this list, **this list wins.**

1. **Woodward et al. 2006, not Moritz & Woodward 2006.** The 2006 BADE paradigm paper is Woodward, Moritz, Cuttler, & Whitman. Woodward is first author. The outline §III uses "Moritz & Woodward (2006)"; that in-text form is incorrect. Use "Woodward et al. 2006" throughout the paper.
2. **Donadi & Hossenfelder 2022 is the Lindblad-form toy model paper, not the path-integral paper.** Two Donadi-Hossenfelder 2022 papers exist; they must not be conflated. The paper cited in §VII is *Toy Model for Local and Deterministic Wave-Function Collapse*, PRA 106, 022212. The companion path-integral paper *A Path Integral over Hilbert Space for Quantum Mechanics*, Annals of Physics 440, 168827, is **not** the framework-relevant citation and is **not** to be cited in §VII.
3. **"Superdeterminism and Retrocausality" (Hossenfelder/Palmer/Price 2022) does not exist as a paper.** It is the 2022 Bonn Workshop and its associated MDPI *Entropy* Special Issue. The peer-reviewed synthesis that emerged from the workshop is Adlam/Hance/Hossenfelder/Palmer 2024, *Taxonomy for Physics Beyond Quantum Mechanics*, Proc. Roy. Soc. A 480: 20230779. **Price is not an author on the Taxonomy paper.** The old ghost citation "Hossenfelder, Palmer, Price 2022" must not appear in the paper.
4. **Clark 2013 authorship.** *Whatever Next?* is Clark, not Friston. Friston is the FEP architect but not the author of this paper.
5. **Sterzer et al. 2018 venue.** *The Predictive Coding Account of Psychosis* is in *Biological Psychiatry*, not *Frontiers*.
6. **Wolpert-Rovelli-Scharnhorst date.** December 2025, not 2026. Hossenfelder's video (March 2026) is secondary coverage, not the primary source.
7. **Elshatlawy et al. date.** April 2025 (v1); v2 June 2025. Not 2026.
8. **Norton 2008 framing.** Norton is a skeptic of strong universal underdetermination, not a supporter. Cite neutrally, not as support for the paper's argument. The paper's argument does not need strong underdetermination; Quine-Duhem holism plus Stanford's new induction suffice.
9. **Rovelli 2024 "Back to Reichenbach" print year.** Listed as 2024 in the foundation memo; Springer confirms print publication is *J Gen Philos Sci* 56 (1): 97-115 (2025), DOI assigned in 2024. Either (2024) or (2025) is defensible; recommend (2025) since that matches the final printed citation. Flag for final submission pass.
10. **Hossenfelder 2024 "Quantum Confusions" is a preprint only.** arXiv:2309.12299, v1 September 2023, v2 October 2024. Not a formal journal publication. Cite as arXiv preprint.
11. **Arsiwalla et al. 2025 → Elshatlawy et al. 2025 author-order correction.** Master-refs v1 [L4-3] header and in-text canonical form reversed the author order of arXiv:2504.16225. Correct order is Elshatlawy, H., Rickles, D., & Arsiwalla, X.D. — confirmed across arXiv v1 abstract page, arXiv v2 PDF (29 June 2025) byline, and the Routledge edited volume *Quantum Gravity and Computation: Information, Pregeometry, and Digital Physics* chapter 4 listing. ## APPLICATION CLUSTER — Superdeterminism Debate (§VII)

### [A-0] Bell, J.S. (1964). *On the Einstein Podolsky Rosen Paradox.* Physics Physique Fizika, 1(3), 195-200.
- **DOI:** 10.1103/PhysicsPhysiqueFizika.1.195
- **In-text:** Bell 1964
- **Status:** `LOCKED`
- **Role:** Structural constraint on local hidden-variable accounts of quantum correlations. The conjunction of assumptions (locality, measurement statistics, statistical independence) the theorem rests on is framework-relevant: experimental violation of the inequality forces revision somewhere in the conjunction without specifying where. §VII opens by establishing the translation-only-zone condition at the foundational level of the QM-interpretation debate through this constraint.
- **Note:** [A-0] numbering reserves foundational position in the application cluster; can be renumbered at submission (continuous A-1 through A-9) if the target venue prefers.

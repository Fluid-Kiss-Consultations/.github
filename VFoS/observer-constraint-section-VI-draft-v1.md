§V closed with the claim that the cross-disciplinary bridge — the claim that what §§II-V's literatures describe is structurally one phenomenon — is unoccupied in the literature. I take up that bridge here.

What §§II-V have each documented, within four methodologically distinct literatures, can be recast as instantiations of a single structural pattern. The recasting is the framework's move; the individual findings carry their own weight on their own terms. §II documents the predictive-processing cognitive architecture: an observer whose access to the world proceeds exclusively through causal mediation and whose model of the world is generated rather than received, with the same precision-weighting machinery that stabilizes perception against noise structurally available to stabilize belief against disconfirmation. §III documents that, in domains whose substrate is not causally contactable, this availability is the cognitive default; clinical psychiatry has operationalized the default as BADE and shown it dimensional across the population. §IV documents that the routing pattern §III describes as cognitively available is also logically permitted — the holism of confirmation (Duhem 1906/1954; Quine 1951) underwrites auxiliary-hypothesis deployment as a structural feature of evidence-theory relations, and Stanford 2006 supplies the community-level empirical record of the pattern. §V documents that fundamental physics, by four independent routes, has converged on observer-dependence at the zone where direct causal contact with the modeled substrate is unavailable — the circularity Wolpert et al. 2025 identify in cosmological reasoning, Rovelli 2025's observer-indexed thermodynamic arrow, Wolfram 2023's observer-theoretic reconstruction of physical law, and Arsiwalla et al. 2025's formal cybernetic observers.

The claim the framework makes is not that these findings are analogous. Analogies identify similarities and stop. The claim is that the four are instantiations of a single structural condition, and that the condition can be specified precisely. The condition is this: an observer whose access to a substrate proceeds exclusively through causal mediation, whose internal construction of that substrate takes the form of a generative predictive model, whose cognitive ecology supplies competing explanatory resources, and whose domain of inquiry does not admit direct causal contact with what the model refers to, inhabits an evidence-routing topology in which disconfirming evidence has structurally more than one locus of possible revision. The topology is a property of the access-plus-construction structure, not a property of any particular substrate or discipline. This is the condition the framework identifies, and §§II-V are what happens when it obtains.

§I previewed the claim that BADE-style self-sealing and theoretical-commitment entrenchment at the foundations of physics are formally identical, promising a precise defense in §VI. By formally identical I mean isomorphic in the topology of how evidence routes through the system. Two evidence-integration structures are topologically identical in this sense when their core commitments stand in the same structural relation to their auxiliary commitments, when disconfirming evidence has the same set of structurally available revision loci, and when the selection of which locus takes the revision is underdetermined by the evidence itself by the same mechanism. A patient with active delusions and a physicist defending a quantum-interpretation commitment, on this reading, are not merely instances of a general human fallibility they happen to share; they are systems routing disconfirming evidence through structurally identical topologies, with the routing permitted logically by the holism of confirmation and executed cognitively by the predictive-processing architecture. What individuates them is not the topology but the substrate, the stakes, and the locus at which the revision comes to rest. The clinical case is individually diagnosable because psychiatric outcome measures make the locus visible; the physics case distributes the same topology across a community whose outcome measures are slower and more contested. The architecture is the same; the phenotype differs.

A reader encountering the claim will reasonably ask why, on this reading, theoretical commitments entrench as hard as they do. The logic of underdetermination, §IV makes clear, only *permits* the routing. The architecture of §II-§III makes the routing cognitively *available*. Neither alone explains the observed asymmetry in how difficult it is to dislodge an entrenched theoretical commitment compared to what the evidential history would predict. Harding et al. 2024 supply the missing component. Their hybrid predictive-coding model distinguishes iterative inference — the moment-to-moment error-driven updating §II described — from amortised inference: pattern-completion drawn from a stored model, feedforward, computationally cheap, structurally resistant to local perturbation. Entrenched theoretical commitments, on the framework's reading, operate through the amortised component. A quantum interpretation is not re-derived each time it is deployed; it supplies the interpretive frame within which new data are already being read. Disconfirming evidence does not confront the commitment iteratively at first contact; it confronts the cached model that feeds forward before iterative processing begins. This is why the evidential history alone underdetermines the rate of entrenchment, and why §II-§IV are jointly necessary but §III's amortisation finding is the structural explanation of *how fast* the routing stabilizes.

The auxiliary-routing pattern has a specific signature in the quantum-interpretation literature, and the signature matches the topology §§II-IV specify. Disputes over the measurement problem, the preferred-basis problem, and the ontological status of the state vector are not adjudicated, in the main, by evidence that forces revision at a single locus. They are adjudicated by redistributing interpretive commitments across auxiliary structures — which entities count as ontologically primary, which measurement-outcome structure is fundamental, which invariance is preserved under which revision. The Everettian's response to Born-rule challenges, the pilot-wave theorist's response to Lorentz-invariance challenges, the objective-collapse theorist's response to pointer-reading concerns: each routes the pressure to a different auxiliary, preserves the central interpretive commitment, and does so coherently within the received holistic frame. This is not a diagnosis of quantum-foundations work as intellectually failing. It is a characterization of the structural mode in which the work, under translation-only-zone conditions, necessarily proceeds. §VII examines one contemporary case in detail: the superdeterminism / retrocausality / standard-QM debate, now formalized taxonomically (Adlam et al. 2024) and visible in Hossenfelder's own instrumentalist turn (Hossenfelder 2024).

A methodology note is due at this point of the argument. The framework treats each layer's empirically-anchored findings as instantiations of a common architectural pattern, and the identification of the four layers as instances of one structural condition extends beyond what any single cited source claims as a cross-disciplinary synthesis. The extension requires explicit acknowledgment. Across §§II-V I flagged eight synthetic moves — the translation-zone default-inversion generalization at §II para 1, the architectural generalization of Sterzer et al. 2018 at §III para 3, the auxiliary-routing characterization of BADE as Duhem-Quine-compatible across §III paras 6-7, the historical-record reading of Stanford 2006 through §II's predictive-processing architecture at §IV para 4, and four readings of physics-side results through the framework's condition at §V paras 2-5 (Wolpert et al., Rovelli, Wolfram, Arsiwalla et al.). Enumeration recites the ledger; aggregation by criterion is cleaner and no less honest. The criterion: each extension reads a layer's finding through the architectural apparatus the adjacent layer developed, and treats the finding as an instantiation of a structural condition that the cited source, on its own terms, does not claim as a cross-disciplinary pattern. The extensions are individually defensible on each layer's terms. The aggregate — the identification of the four layers as instantiations of one structural condition, with the topology of evidence-routing as the identifying invariant — is the framework's synthesis. The sources are not asked to carry that claim; they are asked only to carry what they individually assert.

What remains is the case. §VII takes up the superdeterminism / retrocausality / standard-QM debate as a worked application: a contemporary domain in which the translation-only-zone condition obtains, the generative-and-amortised cognitive architecture is operating at the community level, the holism of confirmation underwrites the auxiliary-routing the architecture executes, and the amortised-commitment machinery entrenches positions the evidence does not directly adjudicate. The framework does not adjudicate which of the interpretations is correct. It specifies what shape the debate necessarily takes, and reads Hossenfelder's instrumentalist turn as a recognizable move from within that shape rather than outside it.

## Structural moves carried in §VI (for changelog / pressure-test reference)

The condition is specified explicitly in para 2: observer whose access is causally mediated, whose model is generatively constructed, whose cognitive ecology supplies competing explanatory resources, and whose domain of inquiry lacks direct causal contact with the substrate — such an observer inhabits an evidence-routing topology with structurally more than one available revision locus.

2. **"Formally identical" IOU paid** (para 3). §I v2's promise that "formally identical" means "isomorphic in the topology of how evidence routes through the system" is defined operationally: same core-plus-auxiliary relations, same set of revision loci, same mechanism of evidence-underdetermination over locus selection. Clinical BADE and physics-foundations theoretical entrenchment share the topology; the phenotype differs by substrate, stakes, and timescale.

3. **Amortised/cached mechanism explains entrenchment rate** (para 4). §II-§IV jointly establish cognitive availability and logical permission for the routing; they do not explain the observed rate of entrenchment. Harding et al. 2024's amortised-inference finding (anchored at §III para 6) is the structural explanation: theoretical commitments are drawn from cached feedforward structure before iterative processing begins, so disconfirming evidence confronts the cache before it confronts the current prediction.

4. **QM-interpretation debate structure isomorphic with framework topology** (para 5). Quantum-foundations debates are characterized as operating through auxiliary redistribution — each interpretive camp preserves its central commitment by routing pressure to a different auxiliary. The characterization matches the topology §§II-IV specify and sets up §VII's specific case.

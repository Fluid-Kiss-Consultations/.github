§VII para 6 flagged the four objections extending the framework to a contemporary live debate naturally provokes: that the diagnostic register pathologizes physicists, that the framework cannot itself be exempt from the architecture it describes, that the framework risks unfalsifiability, and that what is offered is a relabeling of paradigm-stability claims already present in the post-Kuhnian literature. I take these in sequence.

The first objection is that extending clinical-psychiatric vocabulary to physicists pathologizes them. The framework does not make that extension. §VI para 3 established that clinical BADE and community-level theoretical entrenchment share the topology of evidence-routing; what individuates the clinical case from the community case is substrate, stakes, and timescale — not topology. The dimensional foothold the framework relies on is Woodward et al. 2007: the self-sealing mechanism is measurably present across nonclinical populations without any individual being diagnosable. §VII para 3 is explicit on the application to physics: the framework's claim is not that any individual physicist exhibits the clinical-psychiatric mechanism §III operationalizes; the cognitive default in the translation-only zone is the framework's extension of §III's finding, not the finding itself. No diagnostic attribution to any physicist is entailed or intended. The claim is structural, not personal.

The second is that the framework, applied to itself, refutes itself: if all cognition in the translation-only zone exhibits the topology §VI specifies, this paper exhibits it too, and the argument collapses. §I para 4 closed with the response the framework holds to: the cognitive architecture under analysis is the only one available, the framework applies to this paper as cleanly as to any other, and the consequence is not an embarrassment for the argument but the argument itself. Self-application is not self-refutation. The framework does not claim an exemption it then forfeits; it makes a descriptive claim about the architecture and extends that claim to its own production. What the extension requires is auditability — that each synthetic move be flagged, each citation be load-bearing, each extension be individually reviewable on the layer's terms — which the paper's structural-moves and open-flags apparatus operationalize at section level.

The third is that the framework is unfalsifiable: any pattern of disciplinary disagreement can be read as auxiliary-routing, any community resistance as amortised-commitment entrenchment. The framework is not an empirical theory of physics and does not carry falsifiability obligations in that register. It is scope-bounded by §I, §VII para 6, and §IX to the translation-only-zone condition, and within that scope it makes specific structural predictions: rate of theoretical-commitment entrenchment exceeding what evidential history alone would predict; taxonomization as the community-level response to auxiliary-routing density; and recognizable-from-within redistribution of theoretical weight at the translation-only-zone boundary as distinct from retreat from it. The contrapositive — that domains in which direct causal contact with the modeled substrate is available exhibit rapid convergence of the kind the framework does not predict for translation-only zones — is observationally supported by the rest of ordinary scientific practice. Auditability through §§II-V grounding and flagged synthetic extensions is the diagnostic-framework operational substitute for strict empirical falsifiability; the framework is assessable by whether its structural predictions obtain under the scope it specifies.

The fourth is that what the framework offers is a relabeling of paradigm-stability claims already available in the post-Kuhnian literature. Kuhn's account characterizes community-level paradigm dynamics at a historical-sociological register. This framework specifies the cognitive architecture — the predictive-processing generative model of §II, the self-sealing BADE mechanism of §III, the holism-of-confirmation permission of §IV — that the community-level phenomenon supervenes on. The amortised/iterative distinction Harding et al. 2024 supply, read through §VI para 4 as the rate-of-entrenchment specification, is not in Kuhn: Kuhn's account describes that paradigms are sticky; it does not specify which cognitive machinery makes them sticky at the rate they do. The translation-only-zone condition further bounds where the mechanism bites — Kuhn's account is not domain-specific in that sense. The cross-disciplinary bridge §§II-V integrate — predictive-processing neuroscience, clinical psychiatry, philosophy of science, and foundations of physics read as instantiations of a single structural condition — is the framework's specific contribution, and is not available in Kuhn.

These four objections are not an exhaustive list of the pushback a contemporary reader may bring. They are the structurally load-bearing ones — the objections that, if not answered, would defeat the extension §VII makes. §IX tightens the framework's positive and negative claims to closing form.

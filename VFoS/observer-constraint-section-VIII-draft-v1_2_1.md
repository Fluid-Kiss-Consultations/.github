§VII para 6 flagged the four objections extending the framework to a contemporary live debate naturally provokes: that the diagnostic register pathologizes physicists, that the framework cannot itself be exempt from the architecture it describes, that the framework risks unfalsifiability, and that what is offered is a relabeling of paradigm-stability claims already available in the post-Kuhnian literature. I take them in sequence.

The first objection, at its strong form, is not that the paper calls physicists ill — §I para 4 and §III para 5 foreclose that reading — but that the structural-isomorphism claim itself puts foundational-physics theoretical entrenchment in a category with delusional ideation, and no disclaimer at the individual-diagnosis level undoes the categorical placement. The framework concedes the isomorphism claim and specifies what it entails: shared topology of evidence-routing — core commitments standing in the same structural relation to auxiliary commitments, disconfirming evidence facing the same set of revision loci, locus-selection underdetermined by the evidence at the same mechanism. It does not entail shared clinical status, diagnostic descriptor, or treatment relevance. §III para 3 establishes the foothold: the pathology is quantitative, not categorical — whatever produces a clinical case produces, at smaller magnitudes, the ordinary failure of an otherwise competent observer to update a model already settled on. Woodward et al. 2007 cash this empirically at the nonclinical register — the bias against disconfirmatory evidence is measurable across delusion-proneness as a continuous cognitive disposition, without any individual in the sample being clinically diagnosable. §VI para 3 completes the move — the architecture is shared; what individuates the clinical from the community case is substrate, stakes, and timescale. §VII para 3 restates for the application: the community-level cognitive default is the framework's extension of §III's finding, not the finding itself. The topology-shared / substrate-different distinction is not rhetorical softening. It refuses two collapses the objection in either form would force: elision of the seriousness of clinical illness by collapsing the physicist's case into it, and elision of the framework's descriptive reach by shielding the physicist's case from it. The framework extends the architectural machinery, not diagnostic status.

The second is that the framework, applied to itself, refutes itself: if cognition in the translation-only zone exhibits the topology §VI specifies, this paper exhibits it too, and the argument collapses. §I para 4 closed with the response the framework holds to — the cognitive architecture under analysis is the only one available, the framework applies to this paper as cleanly as to any other, and the consequence is not an embarrassment for the argument but the argument itself. Self-application is not self-refutation. The framework makes a descriptive claim about the architecture and extends that claim to its own production rather than claiming an exemption it then forfeits. §I para 2's formulation makes the position explicit: the identity the paper defends is a necessary consequence, given the architecture, of epistemic underdetermination operating on a causally bounded observer. The framework describes the architecture operating under specified conditions, not a privileged observer-position from which the architecture is observed. Self-application is accordingly a prediction the paper makes about itself — the paper's own claims should exhibit the topology §VI specifies, and the question is whether the apparatus by which the paper's claims are made accessible to external scrutiny is equal to that prediction. What the extension requires is auditability at the layer-specific empirical register of §§II-V rather than the framework's unified register, since any attempt by the framework to self-audit from within its own unified register would be the move the framework diagnoses. The apparatus is visible in the published paper: §§II-V ground their claims in externally-verified empirical literatures (predictive-processing meta-analysis, BADE dimensional extension, Duhem-Quine holism and Stanford's historical record, four-strand physics-side observer-dependence convergence); §V para 5 names the cross-disciplinary bridge as the framework's specific contribution rather than any cited source's claim; §VI para 6 explicitly acknowledges the synthetic moves. These are the published-paper operational substitutes for the self-exemption the framework declines.

The third is that the framework is unfalsifiable: any disciplinary pattern can be read as auxiliary-routing, any community resistance as amortised-commitment entrenchment. The framework's claims are physiological at source, not metaphysical — §II para 5 grounds the causal-mediation condition at the neuroscience register, not as a stipulation the framework imposes — and it is not an empirical theory of physics, so it does not carry falsifiability obligations in the register that objection typically invokes. It is scope-bounded by §I, §III para 1, §VII para 6, and §IX to the translation-only-zone condition, and within that scope it makes three structural predictions carrying specified negations. First: rate of theoretical-commitment entrenchment exceeds what a Bayesian-update counterfactual over the same evidential record, absent amortised-commitment entrenchment, would predict — the rate at which iterative inference alone, without the feedforward cached-model contribution Harding et al. 2024 document, would update. Candidate operational proxies — citation-pattern shift over specified time windows, review-paper synthesis-move density, mainstream-acceptance latency of peer-produced alternative models — situate the rate-claim for empirical follow-up; the claim itself is structurally specified, and observed rates at or below baseline would undermine it. Second: communities facing auxiliary-routing density beyond a threshold produce taxonomic formalization of the landscape rather than single-locus adjudication; Adlam et al. 2024 is an instance, and absence of the taxonomic move in such domains would undermine the claim. Third: redistribution of theoretical weight recognizable-from-within the topology is the structurally expected response at the translation-only-zone boundary, with §VII para 5's reading of Hossenfelder's instrumentalist turn as a concrete instance; its negation is a redistribution that exits the topology by appeal to direct-causal-contact evidence adjudicating at a single locus, and the framework predicts no such redistribution occurs within the scope it specifies. Stanford 2006's historical record — the systematic failure of scientific communities to conceive viable theoretical alternatives that later prove viable — supplies the community-level empirical trace the framework predicts the translation-only-zone condition generates, an independent observational anchor for the rate-of-entrenchment claim beyond the Harding et al. 2024 mechanism specification. The contrapositive anchored at §III para 1's polarity — direct-causal-contact domains exhibit rapid convergence of the kind the framework does not predict for translation-only zones — is observationally supported by ordinary experimental practice in synthetic chemistry, controlled pharmacology, and structural engineering. The four-strand independent physics-side convergence §V documents supplies a further empirical check, and the check is non-trivial: §V para 3 notes that the physics literature is not merely compatible with the framework's condition but independently formalizes it. Had fundamental physics, along four methodologically distinct routes, not converged on observer-dependence at the zone §IV identifies, the framework's claim that this is the structurally expected response would be undermined. The framework carries assessable predictions, and its scope-claim is testable against the contrapositive and against the physics-side literature independently.

The fourth is that what the framework offers is a relabeling of paradigm-stability claims already available in the post-Kuhnian literature. Kuhn (1962) characterizes community-level paradigm dynamics at a historical-sociological register; subsequent cognitive-science-of-science work (e.g., Nersessian 2008) has characterized paradigm stability at intermediate cognitive-psychological levels. The framework positions within that lineage by supplying the specific cognitive-architectural substrate those characterizations operate over — the predictive-processing generative model of §II, the self-sealing BADE mechanism of §III with its dimensional empirical foothold in Woodward et al. 2007, and the amortised-inference specification Harding et al. 2024 provides for rate of entrenchment. Kuhn's account gestures at cognitive-structural features — gestalt switches, paradigm-indexed training — that make paradigms sticky, but it does not specify the architectural substrate (predictive processing + amortised inference) that fixes the rate at which they stick, and it does not distinguish domains where the mechanism bites sharply (translation-only zones) from domains where it does not (direct-causal-contact practice). The translation-only-zone bounding §III para 1 installs is not available in Kuhn. The mechanism-specification §III para 3 supplies — Sterzer et al. 2018's architectural account of BADE as a distribution of precision weights, rather than a novel failure mode grafted on — distinguishes the framework's register from intentional-level accounts at a level Kuhn and the post-Kuhnian cognitive-science-of-science lineage have engaged obliquely. Intentional-level accounts (motivated reasoning, professional incentive, institutional pressure) generate rate predictions of their own via mechanisms distinct from the amortised-commitment architecture; the amortised-rate prediction the framework generates, by contrast, is not derivable from those supplementations of Kuhn's account, which would still need the architectural substrate the framework supplies to generate the rate-claim Harding et al. 2024 specifically underwrites. The cross-disciplinary bridge §§II-V integrate — predictive-processing neuroscience, clinical psychiatry's dimensional findings, philosophy of science's holism-plus-historical-record, and fundamental physics' four independent routes to observer-dependence — is the framework's specific contribution and is not available in Kuhn or in the post-Kuhnian cognitive-science-of-science literature individually. The framework is a subspecies-specification within a broader entrenchment phenomenon, mechanism-grounded at a level those literatures have not reached.

These four objections do not exhaust the pushback a contemporary reader may bring. They are the structurally load-bearing ones — the objections that, if not answered, defeat the extension §VII makes. Metaphor-not-formal, just-underdetermination, and realism-vs-antirealism objections are addressed implicitly by §VI para 3, §IV paras 5-6 plus §VI para 4, and scope-bounding at §I / §VII para 6 / §IX respectively. §IX crystallizes the framework's positive claim — what it is — and the negative-claim cluster §VIII paras 2-3 have pre-staged — what it is not — into closing form.
